# FinanceHome Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: March 05, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

FinanceHome ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal and financial information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your data when you use FinanceHome, a publicly accessible personal finance management application hosted on Google Cloud Platform (GCP).

By creating an account and using FinanceHome, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- Account credentials: email address and password (stored as a secure hash)
- Profile information you optionally provide during onboarding
- Consent acknowledgments and preferences

### 2.2 Financial Information via Plaid

FinanceHome uses Plaid Technologies, Inc. ("Plaid") to connect to your financial institution accounts. When you link a bank account, Plaid collects and provides us with:

- Account balances and transaction history
- Account names, numbers (last four digits), and institution names
- Account type and status information

We do not receive or store your full bank account credentials. Authentication is handled directly by Plaid. Plaid's privacy policy is available at https://plaid.com/legal/privacy-policy.

### 2.3 Automatically Collected Information

- IP address and approximate geographic location
- Browser type, operating system, and device information
- Log data including pages visited, timestamps, and error events
- Cookies and session tokens required for authentication

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- To provide, operate, and maintain the FinanceHome application
- To display your financial account data and transaction history
- To authenticate your identity and maintain session security
- To generate audit logs for security and compliance purposes
- To respond to your support requests or inquiries
- To detect, prevent, and investigate fraud or unauthorized access
- To comply with legal obligations and Plaid's API requirements

We do not use your financial data to serve advertisements, build marketing profiles, or sell insights to third parties.

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal or financial information. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

### 4.1 Service Providers

We share data with third-party service providers that help us operate the application, including:

- **Plaid Technologies, Inc.** — financial account connectivity
- **Google Cloud Platform (GCP)** — cloud hosting and infrastructure
- **Supabase** — managed PostgreSQL database hosting

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and may only use it to perform services on our behalf.

### 4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of FinanceHome, our users, or the public.

### 4.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred. We will notify you via email before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal and financial data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate security purposes (such as fraud investigation logs, retained for up to 90 days).

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at steverelativity@gmail.com.

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security controls to protect your information, including:

- Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest
- Password hashing using bcrypt
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) support
- Rate limiting on authentication endpoints
- HTTP security headers (HSTS, CSP, X-Frame-Options, etc.)
- Audit logging of sensitive user actions
- Role-based access controls limiting administrative access

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

- **Access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal data
- **Portability:** Request your data in a portable format
- **Revocation:** Disconnect your linked bank accounts at any time via account settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at steverelativity@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

## 8. Cookies

FinanceHome uses session cookies strictly for authentication and security purposes. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

## 9. Children's Privacy

FinanceHome is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us at steverelativity@gmail.com.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email or by posting a notice within the application at least 14 days before the changes take effect.
